\section{Isomorphism}

When two graphs have the exact same structure.

\[ G_1 = (V_1,E_1) \qquad \cong \qquad   G_2=(V_2,E_2) \]

\[ f : V_1 \longleftrightarrow V_2 \]

\[ \{ f(x), f(y) \} \in E_2 \qquad \Leftrightarrow \qquad \{ x, y \} \in  E_1 \]
